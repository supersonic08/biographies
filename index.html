The Genre

Biography/Autobiography

A biography is a story of someone's life being told by someone else. While an autobiography is a story being told by its subject. Biographies tend to be more accurate because they are written later on and by a more objective party. I chose to read two biographies.

Alex.H

Book 1 - Alexander Hamilton: His Life, Our History

Alex had a poor life when he was little, and his mom couldn't teach him much. She died of yellow fever but then he kept on writing & fought in the Revoltionary War. He got married with a woman named Elizabeth Schulyer, AKA: Eliza or Betsey. Her father Phillip came from a wealthy family; he was a general in Washington's Continental Army. Eliza and Alexander had seven kids.

After the war, Hamliton becam a lawyer and talked with other lawyers, too. He had some ideas and helped bring a federal government and the Constitution. Alexander had a fight with someone he disliked - Aaron Burr. He'd thrown away his shot but got shot at. Alexander Hamilton, our Fouding Father, was the greatest writer of all time.

M.Ali

Book 2: The Greatest Muhammed Ali

Muhammad Ali was a man who, at first, wanted revenge, but he became more than that. He was a man of courage. Ali was trained in boxing and learned some new skills and got better every day. Then, he became really good and went to the Olympics with his trainer Archie Moore. He won the Olympics. He faced tough opponents and sometimes lost, but he did not give up and came back and stepped back up. He beat them in the end.

He also stood up for black people in the Civil Rights Movement and protested the Vietnam war and wouldn't go in the draft. He felt blacks should stand up against tyranny. He was definitely "The Greatest."
